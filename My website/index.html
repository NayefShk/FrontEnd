How MINDEASE Works

Three Simple Steps to Better Mental Health



1. Talk Freely
Share your thoughts, fears, or daily struggles with MindEase. Our AI listens without judgment, 24/7.

2. Get Personalized Insights
MindEase analyzes conversations to offer tailored tips, coping strategies, and actionable advice.

3. Stay Safe & Supported
In critical situations, MindEase activates safety protocols, connecting you to emergency contacts or nearby therapists.

Features

1. End-of-Conversation Tips & Action Plans
- After every chat, receive personalized suggestions (e.g., breathing exercises, journaling prompts).
- Curated resources like podcasts, articles, or local support groups based on your needs.

2. Suicide Risk Detection Meter
- Proprietary AI monitors conversation patterns for signs of severe distress or suicidal ideation.
- Triggers immediate support option: emergency contacts, crisis hotlines, or therapist referrals.

Additional Features
- 24/7 Availability: Chat anytime, anywhere.
- Progress Tracking: Visualize your emotional trends over time (optional opt-in).
- Therapist Matching: Get matched with licensed professionals nearby if needed.
